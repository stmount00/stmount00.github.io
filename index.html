Slalom Skiing Analysis

Using Dartfish video editor, I was able to draw angles displaying biomechanical aspects of each turn with freeze frames, and overlay an audio recording to provide analytic commentary through this video clip.

Future Applications in Kinesiology Tech: Force Myography

Feel free to download the PDF of my final paper for this course, where I discuss the prospect of Force Myography (FMG) as a viable, cost-effective alternative for surface electromyography in wearable tech. The application of this technology will allow fitness enthusiasts to monitor fatigue and optimize performance without the need of professional analytic techniques.
